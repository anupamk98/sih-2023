DONATE US

We humbly request your support for SAHAYAKS, our organization dedicated to cleaning and recycling in Kerala. Your generous donations help us make a significant impact in our mission to create a cleaner and more sustainable environment. As a token of our gratitude, for every 10,000 rupees donated, we organize massive clean-up drives in public places across Kerala. Your contribution not only aids in our efforts to promote responsible waste management but also directly contributes to the cleanliness of our shared spaces. Moreover,any excess donation funds are judiciously utilized to enhance our community initiatives. These include installing dustbins in public places, organizing awareness campaigns for cleanliness in schools and colleges, and fostering a culture of environmental stewardship. Together, we can make a tangible difference in the well-being of our communities and the preservation of our beautiful state. Join hands with SAHAYAKS today and be a part of the positive change we're striving to create. Donate now by clicking the DONATE NOW button below and help us pave the way towards a cleaner and greener Kerala.

VOLUNTEER

Calling all passionate individuals who share our commitment to a cleaner and greener Kerala! SAHAYAKS is in need of dedicated volunteers to join our clean-up drives and other community initiatives. Your time and effort can make a remarkable impact in creating a more sustainable environment and fostering a culture of cleanliness. Whether you're a student, a professional, or simply someone who cares deeply about our beautiful state, we welcome you to be a part of our mission.

As a SAHAYAKS volunteer, not only will you have the satisfaction of contributing to a cleaner Kerala, but you'll also receive certains rewards from our side. These include :

  • An appreciation certificate (View Certificate) from us for each clean-up drive you participate in.
  • We also reward your dedication with points in your SAHAYAKS account. These points can be redeemed for a variety of attractive goodies (View Goodies)
